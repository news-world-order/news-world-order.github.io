Do politicians actually represent the voice of the people, or is their message heavily manipulated by other special interests?

Even Barack Obama, an internationally well regarded president, was heavily influenced by the financial and tech industries. It is easy to forget this fact in light of the current US political landscape. In this video installation, we explore this question of representation, especially as technologies such as artificial intelligence are projected to bring about a greater wealth divide between the public and the powerful.
